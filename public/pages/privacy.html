Privacy at Skynet Nexus News

Family-first also means privacy-first. Here is exactly what we collect, what we do not, and how to remove your account.

TL;DR

  • We do not track kids. No analytics run on kid profiles. No ad tech, ever.
  • Parents opt in to an account. We store only email, display name, and a bcrypt hash of the password.
  • Kid profiles store only what you type: a name, birth year, an emoji, a color.
  • Age-appropriate filtering happens in your browser. We never see which stories your kid read.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data. Ever.
  • You can delete your account (and every kid profile with it) any time from the profile page.

What we collect

Parent account: email address, display name, hashed password, an avatar color you pick, timestamps of account creation and last login.

Kid profiles: first name (or nickname), birth year (used only to choose an age band on your device), an emoji, a color. That is the entire list.

Newsletter (optional): email address only, if you sign up on the homepage.

Session cookie: one first-party HttpOnly cookie named skynet.sid so you stay signed in. No tracking pixels, no third-party cookies, no fingerprinting.

COPPA (US kids under 13)

Skynet Nexus News is designed for co-reading by families. Kid profiles exist so a parent can choose what content their child sees at a glance. We treat every kid profile as belonging to the parent account it lives under. That means:

  • Only the parent creates, edits, or deletes a kid profile.
  • Kid profile birth year never leaves your browser except to be stored on our server for you.
  • We never target advertising to any user, adult or child.
  • We never sell any data to any third party.
  • Parents can email privacy@skynet-nexus.local to review or delete any data associated with their account.

How we store data

All account data lives in a single SQLite database on the server, encrypted at rest at the hosting layer. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost 12). Session cookies are HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax, and Secure over HTTPS.

Deleting your data

Sign in, open the Your Family page, scroll to the bottom, and click Delete account. That removes your account, every kid profile, and every session. It is immediate and cannot be undone.

You can also email privacy@skynet-nexus.local and we will delete everything within 30 days.

Analytics & ads

Zero third-party analytics. Zero ads. Zero trackers. Free forever, funded by belief, not by your attention.

Questions

Email privacy@skynet-nexus.local or use the Contact page. A human replies.

Last updated: 2026-07-01. When we change how we handle data, we update this page and note the change here.
